A 3D puzzle game that tests spatial reasoning under time pressure. Rotate a
cluster of blocks step by step to match a target shape before the clock (and
your move budget) runs out. Features three difficulty tiers, a global
leaderboard, and a scoring system that rewards efficient solutions over brute
force.
