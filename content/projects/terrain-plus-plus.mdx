A real-time procedural terrain generator built in C++ and OpenGL. Layered
fbm noise drives the base heightmap, with tunable octaves, frequency,
amplitude, and lacunarity, followed by a thermal erosion pass to carve
realistic ridgelines and scree slopes. An ImGui control panel exposes every
parameter live, including per-layer snow, stone, and grass tinting, so you
can iterate on a landscape in real time.
